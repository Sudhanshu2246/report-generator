Lifestyle Guidance
Based on your functional assessment, we recommend focusing on dietary
modifications to support microbiome balance. Reduce intake of simple
sugars, refined carbohydrates, and alcohol, as these can promote yeast overgrowth.
Emphasize fiber-rich vegetables, fermented foods, and adequate protein to support
beneficial bacterial populations. Consider spacing meals to allow digestive rest between
eating periods. Your elevated sleep score suggests circadian rhythm support is important
- aim for consistent sleep and wake times, reduce evening light exposure, and create a
calming pre-sleep routine. The borderline gut-brain stress indicator points to the value
of stress management techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or gentle movement.
Maintain adequate hydration throughout the day, particularly with filtered water
and herbal teas. Your medication impact score is favorable, suggesting current
medications are not significantly interfering with gut function. Regular movement and
outdoor time can support both sleep quality and stress reduction.
Key Insight
Small, consistent changes often produce the most sustainable results. Focus on one or two areas at a time rather than attempting to change everything at once.
